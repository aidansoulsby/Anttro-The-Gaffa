The Anttro loop

Raise it. Action it. Close it.

Three stages. One closed loop. Every problem tracked from the moment it is raised to the moment it is solved.

Scroll to explore

Stage 01

SnapShot.

Someone scans a QR code. That's it.

Workers raise problems directly, in their own words. No login, no training, no app to download. The Gaffa captures the full picture and notifies the manager immediately.

  • QR code poster in your operation
  • Raise a problem in 60 seconds
  • The Gaffa captures the full picture
  • Cost and time impact calculated automatically
  • Manager notified with a full summary
  • No login or app needed
SnapShot
Stage 02

ActionPlan.

The Gaffa builds the plan with you.

No blank page. The Gaffa walks the manager through triage, scores actions by effort and impact, captures owners and review dates, and sets automatic reminders.

  • Triage built in: size the problem first
  • Actions scored by effort and impact
  • Owners and review dates captured
  • Automatic reminders as dates approach
  • Full action list visible on the dashboard
  • Nothing falls through the cracks
ActionPlan
Stage 03

ReShot.

Loop closed. Or a new cycle begins.

At the review date, the loop closes with value captured. If the fix didn't hold, a new ActionPlan cycle starts automatically. Every loop has a full audit trail.

  • Action-by-action review at close
  • Value recovered logged across five categories
  • Loop closed with a pound figure attached
  • If unresolved: new cycle triggered automatically
  • Full audit trail from raise to close
  • Insights page updated in real time
