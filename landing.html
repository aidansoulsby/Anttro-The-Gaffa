The Anttro loop

Raise it. Action it. Close it.

Three stages. One closed loop. Every problem tracked from the moment it's raised to the moment it's solved. The structure does the thinking, so your managers don't have to carry it in their heads.

Stage 01
SnapShot
Raise it.

A worker scans a QR code. That's it.

  • QR poster on the shop floor. Scan to raise.
  • The Gaffa captures the full picture through conversation
  • Projected cost and time impact surfaced automatically
  • Manager notified immediately with a full summary
ActionPlan triggered
Stage 02
ActionPlan
Action it.

The Gaffa builds the plan with you.

  • Triage built in: size the problem, pick the right approach
  • Actions scored by effort and impact
  • Owners, review dates, and value category captured
  • Automatic reminders as the review date approaches
ReShot triggered on review date
Stage 03
ReShot
Close it.

Loop closed. Or a new cycle begins.

  • Action-by-action review: what worked, what didn't
  • Loop closed with value captured (Safety, Quality, Productivity, People, Cost)
  • If unresolved: new ActionPlan cycle triggered automatically
  • Full audit trail from raise to close
