The Anttro loop

Raise it. Action it. Close it.

Three stages. One closed loop. Every problem tracked from the moment it is raised to the moment it is solved.

SnapShotRaise it. ActionPlanAction it. ReShotClose it.
Stage 01

Someone scans a QR code. That's it.

Workers raise problems directly, in their own words. No login, no training. The Gaffa captures the full picture through conversation and notifies the manager immediately.

  • QR code poster in your operation. Scan to raise.
  • The Gaffa captures the full picture through conversation
  • Projected cost and time impact surfaced automatically
  • Manager notified immediately with a full summary
Stage 02

The Gaffa builds the plan with you.

No blank page. The Gaffa walks the manager through triage, scores actions by effort and impact, captures owners and review dates, and sets automatic reminders.

  • Triage built in: size the problem, pick the right approach
  • Actions scored by effort and impact
  • Owners, review dates, and value category captured
  • Automatic reminders as the review date approaches
Stage 03

Loop closed. Or a new cycle begins.

At the review date, the loop closes with value captured. If the problem persists, a new ActionPlan cycle starts automatically. Full audit trail from raise to close.

  • Action-by-action review: what worked, what did not
  • Loop closed with value captured across five categories
  • If unresolved: new ActionPlan cycle triggered automatically
  • Full audit trail from raise to close
